I'm Sarisma, a people person who thrives on interactions. My days are filled with reading to understand others, writing to express my voice, and speaking to articulate my opinions. Currently, I proudly wear the hat of a freelance writer and host, turning my passion into a profession.

Driven by my love for humanity and education, I've actively engaged with diverse organizations and projects, aiming to elevate the educational landscape and foster skill development.

But that's not all; my heart lies with Bridge-Ed, a heartfelt initiative I founded. With it, I'm on a mission to empower government schools in Lumbini Province, bridging the gap between students and skillful quality education.

Join me in this transformative journey as we create a brighter future for the young minds of Nepal!
